Who we are

Sonya Tiratsuyan: Founder

Sonya started cooking on her own at the ripe young age of 9: simple roast chicken soon evolved into more complex dishes, and she began experimenting in the kitchen. Sonya knew she wanted to be a teacher after her very first day of pre-school, and her passion for food seemed the perfect match.

Joe Smith: Chef

Joe cooked in a professional kitchen for several years before becoming a teacher. Soon after beginning his teaching career, he decided he'd much rather help the every-day-chef cook great food at home.

Tina Belcher: Chef

Tina's dad had always owned a little burger place, where she quickly learned that burgers are essential to human existence. Being in charge of the burger of the day, Tina brings huge creativity to the kitchen.
