Who we are

Sonya Tiratsuyan: Founder

Sonya was always in the kitchen with her grandmother, almost always elbow deep in food. She started cooking on her own at the ripe young age of 9: simple roast chicken soon evolved into more complex dishes, and she began experimenting with sauces, textures, and unique flavors. Sonya knew she wanted to be a teacher after her very first day of pre-school, and her passion for food seemed the perfect match.
